      How to Play

      Setting

      You are a newcomer to a primal band of eight founders — hunters, healers, elders, wanderers. There is no language yet for "future," no walls, no tamed fire. Just hunger, thirst, cold, and each other. Survive and watch a civilization emerge from scratch.

      Controls

      • W A S D / arrows — Walk
      • T — Talk to nearest founder
      • F — Attack nearest target
      • E — Pick up items nearby
      • I — Open/close inventory
      • Tab — Show the band roster
      • ? — This help panel
      • Esc — Close any modal

      Survival

      Your four needs decay over time. Food drops unless you forage berries or eat from your inventory. Water drops unless you drink from the river. Social drops unless you talk to other founders. Health drops when any other need bottoms out — and when bears attack.

      Conversations

      The right sidebar lists every active and recent conversation. The bar at the bottom shows the messages of your current one. NPCs think between turns; give them a moment to reply.
